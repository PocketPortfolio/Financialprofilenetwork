## Notes

- **Dev.to:** Canonical URL field is usually at the bottom of the edit form, under "Advanced Settings" or "SEO Settings"
- **CoderLegion:** May be under "Post Settings" or "Advanced Options" - if not available, add it as a note in the post or contact support
- After setting canonical URLs, wait 24-48 hours for search engines to recognize the change
- Monitor Google Search Console for indexing updates
