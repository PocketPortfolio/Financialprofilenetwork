OpenBrokerCSV: the open standard for broker trade CSVs

A simple, strict, and compatible CSV schema for portfolio trades across brokers — built for fast imports, accurate P/L, and privacy.

Schema (columns & rules)

Required columns

  • broker, trade_id, timestamp (ISO 8601)
  • symbol, side [BUY|SELL]
  • quantity (number), price (number)
  • trade_currency (ISO 4217)

Optional columns

  • fee (number)
  • settlement_currency (ISO 4217)
  • fx_rate (number)

Unknown columns are preserved during normalization (prefixed or passed through).

FAQ

Why not XLSX?

CSV is simpler, stable across tools, and easier to validate in CI/CD.

Are API imports supported?

Yes in future. For now, CSV keeps things portable across brokers.

How are fees handled?

Include a numeric fee column; our importer subtracts fees in P/L.
