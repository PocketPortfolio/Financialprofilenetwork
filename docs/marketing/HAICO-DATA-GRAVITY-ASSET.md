## What the mandate asked for vs tooling

The **10-second looping GIF** (legacy pipe → sovereign pivot → stateless dissolve) is **motion design**: After Effects, Rive, Lottie export, or GenAI video (Runway / Veo) using the phase script from the HAICO brief.

This environment **does not output `.gif` or video loops** in one step. The schematic above **implements the same narrative in one still** so GTM isn’t blocked.

## Handoff to motion

1. Use `data-gravity-sovereign-schematic.png` as **layout and color key** (slate, amber flow, emerald shield; payload sliver is **amber**, not hyperscaler blue).
2. Animate the three beats from the HAICO brief (0–3s / 3–6s / 6–10s loop).
3. Export **GIF** (under ~8MB for LinkedIn) or **MP4** (often better quality); LinkedIn accepts both for feed posts.
