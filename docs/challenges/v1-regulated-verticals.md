# 🏆 Design Partnership Challenge: Secure the Sovereign Frontier 🏆

**Roles:** AI Architects, Product Designers, Full Stack Engineers, Software Engineers, and Founders—or enter if you believe you have what it takes.

## The paradigm shift
Pocket Portfolio is **sovereign infrastructure**: the privacy layer organisations need to deploy and use AI safely in regulated environments. Our consumer terminal proves the architecture with thousands of privacy-conscious investors. Our infrastructure node package is seeing a surge in downloads as developers expand the sovereign ecosystem. Experts at forums are voting for our paradigm shift as a new way of thinking and systems design.

## The challenge
This is an **independent framework implementation** exercise. Use Pocket Portfolio sovereign architecture to design a new mode of operation in your vertical—**Healthcare**, **Defense**, **Finance**, **Energy**, or another regulated domain.

**Submit:** strategy doc, wireframe or prototype (screenshots or URL), a full-stack fork of this boilerplate, and adversarial feedback—on the **GitHub Discussions** thread below. Open review with the advisory committee and early users.

## What you get
- Your name or logo as an **early design partner** on flagship pages.
- Early access to sovereign innovation.
- If a submission reaches revenue modelling, an option to **lead that vertical** (terms to be discussed).

### Overview
This repository is the boilerplate for the **Pocket Portfolio Design Partnership Challenge**. We are recruiting architects to extend our **limited-scope processor** posture into adjacent regulated industries—Healthcare, Defense, Finance, Energy—with evidence, not narrative alone.

Public hub: https://www.pocketportfolio.app/designchallenge

### What to submit
Reply to this discussion with:

- **Fork link** — public fork using the sovereign importer SDK where relevant.
- **Strategy** — Markdown or PDF on regulatory scope reduction for your vertical.
- **Prototype** — screenshots or a hosted staging URL showing sovereign UX in practice.

### Review
Submissions are reviewed by the **Pocket Portfolio advisory committee** and early institutional users. Feedback stays on-thread for transparency.

### Engineering expectations
Challenge forks should document sovereign boundaries explicitly: what stays on-device, what crosses the wire, and how AI calls remain stateless. Broker-adjacent flows may reference the importer; other verticals should map equivalent scope-reduction patterns.
